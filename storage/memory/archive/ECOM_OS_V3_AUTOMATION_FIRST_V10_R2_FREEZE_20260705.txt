5. SCREEN AUTOMATION MODEL

00 Home:
- Product Intake;
- Source, Photo and Receipt agents;
- Product Passport;
- Compliance precheck;
- Radar and Finance context;
- automatic Route01/Route02 selection.

01 New Product:
- title, category, item specifics, photo checks, duplicate checks, price, stock, ads, shipping, Radar, Finance and draft package are automatic;
- operator reviews preview, fixes blockers and confirms exact Draft gate.

02 Variants:
- axes, Variant Matrix, SKU, EAN, photos, prices, stock and parent/variant package are automatic;
- operator resolves ambiguous axes and conflicts.

03 Listing Studio:
- Internal Marketplace Search;
- approved External Web Research;
- AI/LLM Semantic Research;
- Evidence and policy validation;
- one Canonical Listing Package;
- package hash and Route07 request.

04 Monitoring:
- READONLY marketplace snapshots;
- sales, returns, delivery, ads and lifecycle events;
- Finance/Stock updates;
- exceptions to Route07.
Current status is UI model only until fresh marketplace READONLY integration exists.

05 Finance and Stock:
- receipts, purchase cost, shipping, fees, ads, returns, stock, profit, reserve and EÜR are reconciled automatically;
- operator handles exceptions only.

06 Radar:
- automatic Evidence and Market Intelligence Hub;
- inputs from Home, Route01, Route02, Route03 and Route05;
- three channels: Internal Marketplace, approved External Web and AI/LLM;
- evaluates confidence, freshness, conflicts, source count, evidence count and marketplace passport;
- updates Evidence Registry;
- automatically distributes verified results to Home, Route01, Route02, Route03, Route05 and Route07;
- operator only adds evidence, resolves conflicts or approves a connector;
- Radar never publishes, revises or invents market facts.

07 Decision Center:
- aggregates package hash, evidence, policy, risks and blockers;
- Ion approves, rejects or sends to rework;
- Route07 never performs marketplace write;
- Draft, Publish, Revise and Delete remain separate exact gates.

08 Memory and Archive:
- immutable snapshots, manifest, hashes, reports, lessons, diffs and rollback metadata should save automatically;
- operator searches history, creates handoff or requests restoration;
- delete remains a separate destructive gate.
