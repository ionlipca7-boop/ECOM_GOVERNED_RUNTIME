DATA RULES
- Illustrative search numbers are not facts.
- If exact volume is unavailable, store index, rank, change, proxy method and confidence.
- Do not directly add Google index, eBay sold count and forum mentions.
- Normalize each source to its own 0–100 score with confidence before aggregation.
- Raw evidence is immutable.
- Normalized score may be recalculated.
- Every decision has a version.
- BUY/TEST/WATCH/REJECT must reference data window, formula version, source set, confidence, operator approval and factual test result.
- Primary market Germany; primary language German.
- Preserve synonyms, spelling variants, English product terms, sizes, power, connectors, models and compatibility.

FULL DATA CHAIN
raw signals -> source adapters -> query normalization -> intent classification -> problem/product/marketplace streams -> time series -> channel map -> aggregation -> competition/profit/risk -> weekly map -> product hunter -> supplier hunter -> market action -> test -> sales feedback -> learning.

INTENT CLASSES
Informational, problem, solution, concrete product and readiness to buy.

WEEKLY OUTPUT
TOP-100, rising, falling, new, bought, low competition and best margin.

ADVANTAGE SIGNALS
Weak supply, poor competitor reviews, missing variants, long delivery, weak listing cards, price gap and useful bundles.

MARKET ENTRY OUTPUT
