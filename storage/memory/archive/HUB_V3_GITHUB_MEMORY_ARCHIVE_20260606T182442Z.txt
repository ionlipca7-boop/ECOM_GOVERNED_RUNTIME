Status: SAVED

## Language rules

Ion-facing Telegram output: Russian only.
This applies to menus, cards, reports, previews, warnings and approval packets.

Buyer-facing eBay content for Germany: German only.
This applies to title, description, item specifics text, image text, shop block text and related product text.

Supplier pages may be English, Chinese or another language, but final buyer content must be German.
If image text is not German, the image must be flagged for rework before final listing use.

## Shop block rule

Routes 01 and 02 must support a German shop/related-products block in the eBay description.
Route 03 will later optimize this block.

The block can show:
- compatible products;
- accessories;
- replacements;
- upgrades;
- bundles;
- alternatives;
- selected extra products from the shop.

Rules:
- buyer text German only;
- Ion preview Russian only;
- only truthful compatibility;
- no broken product reference;
- do not distract from the main product;
- every shown item needs a clear reason.
