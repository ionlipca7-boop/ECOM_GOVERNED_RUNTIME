## Purpose

Route 05 is the money and stock control center.
It must know what was bought, what is in stock, what sold, what returned, what cost money and what produced profit.

## Two cores

05A Stock core:
- product stock;
- SKU and variant stock;
- marketplace stock links;
- sold quantity;
- available quantity;
- low stock alerts;
- reorder decision.

05B Finance core:
- purchase cost;
- shipping cost;
- packaging cost;
- marketplace fees;
- ad cost;
- return cost;
- refund cost;
- profit before reserve;
- profit after reserve;
- date-based reports.
