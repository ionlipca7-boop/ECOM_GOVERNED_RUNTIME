## Telegram reports for Ion

All reports must be Russian.
Reports must be simple:
- what came in;
- what went out;
- what is left;
- what was sold;
- what returned;
- what profit/loss exists;
- what needs Ion decision.

## Buyer content

Route 05 does not create buyer-facing listing content.
It only supplies cost, stock and finance truth to other routes.
