## Internal sources

Route 06 receives signals from:
- Route 01 product attempts;
- Route 02 variant and bundle ideas;
- Route 03 listing experiments;
- Route 04 weak or strong listing life;
- Route 05 stock, profit and reorder signals;
- Route 07 Ion decisions;

## External source groups

- marketplace search signals;
- competitor listings;
- price ranges;
- sold or performance evidence when legally available;
- supplier pages and screenshots;
- supplier costs;
- reviews and defect evidence;
- trend/search interest tools;
- official marketplace APIs where available.

## Rule

All external data must be marked by source and confidence.
Supplier claims are not truth until evidence confirms them.
