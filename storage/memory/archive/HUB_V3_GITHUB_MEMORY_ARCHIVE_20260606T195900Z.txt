## Product radar score

Each opportunity gets a score, not a blind yes/no.

Score blocks:
- demand strength
- competition pressure
- price room
- cost clarity
- profit after reserve
- stock risk
- return risk
- defect risk
- compliance risk
- photo/content possibility
- German listing fit
- supplier trust
- bundle or variant potential
- route fit: 01, 02 or 03

## Verdicts

- BUY_TEST_SMALL
- WATCH_ONLY
- NEEDS_MORE_EVIDENCE
- SEND_TO_ROUTE_01
- SEND_TO_ROUTE_02
- SEND_TO_ROUTE_03
- SEND_TO_ROUTE_05_REORDER
- BLOCKED_RISK

## Rule

No opportunity becomes green without profit after reserve and compliance review.
